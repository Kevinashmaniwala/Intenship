# Diabetes Patient Health & Risk Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

The **Diabetes Patient Health & Risk Analysis Dashboard** provides insights into patient health metrics and diabetes risk patterns using data visualization.

This dashboard helps analyze the relationship between **age, glucose level, BMI, insulin, and diabetes status** to identify trends and high-risk patients.

The goal of this project is to demonstrate **data analysis, visualization skills, and dashboard design using healthcare data**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze the distribution of **diabetes vs non-diabetes patients**
* Identify **high-risk patients** based on health indicators
* Understand **age-wise diabetes prevalence**
* Explore the relationship between **glucose and insulin levels**
* Study **BMI trends across age groups**
* Provide interactive insights using filters

---

## 📁 Dataset Information

The dataset contains medical diagnostic measurements used to predict diabetes risk.

Key attributes include:

* **Age** – Age of the patient
* **Glucose** – Plasma glucose concentration
* **BMI** – Body Mass Index
* **Insulin** – Insulin level in blood
* **Outcome** – Diabetes status (Diabetic / Non-Diabetic)

## 📊 Key Insights

* A significant portion of patients show **high glucose levels**, indicating increased diabetes risk.
* **Middle-aged and older groups** show higher diabetes prevalence.
* Higher **BMI values** tend to correlate with increased diabetes risk.
* A relationship between **glucose and insulin levels** can be observed among diabetic patients.
