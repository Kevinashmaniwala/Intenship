

# 📱 Google Play Store Data Analysis & Strategic Insights

**Project Type:** Market Research & Data Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a detailed analysis of the Google Play Store landscape. The goal was to transform raw, messy app data into an executive-level **Power BI Dashboard** to understand market trends, category dominance, and user engagement patterns.

## 🛠️ Data Engineering & Cleaning

Since the raw dataset contained many inconsistencies, I performed extensive data cleaning to ensure accuracy:

* **Metric Conversion:** Transformed 'Installs' and 'Price' from text (e.g., "10,000+", "$4.99") into clean numerical values for calculation.
* **Size Standardization:** Converted app sizes from "M" (Megabytes) and "k" (kilobytes) into a single uniform scale.
* **Handling Nulls:** Managed missing values in 'Ratings' and 'Content Ratings' to prevent skewed results.
* **Deduplication:** Removed redundant app entries to maintain a unique dataset.

## 📊 Business Intelligence Dashboard

The final report is a professional **Navy Blue & Gold** themed dashboard designed for quick decision-making.

### **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**

* **Market Scale:** Total Apps, Total Reviews, and Overall Average Rating.
* **Category Analysis:** Discovering which categories (Games, Family, Tools) have the highest market share.
* **Monetization Strategy:** Comparison between **Free vs. Paid** apps and their impact on user ratings.
* **Audience Segmentation:** Breakdown of apps by **Content Rating** (Everyone, Teen, Mature 17+).
* **User Sentiments:** Analyzing the relationship between the number of reviews and the final app rating.

## 🚀 Key Takeaways

1. **Top Categories:** Identified which genres drive the most user engagement.
2. **Size vs. Success:** Analyzed if larger apps tend to have lower ratings due to performance issues.
3. **Pricing Trends:** Found that certain categories perform better as paid apps compared to others.

## 💻 Tech Stack

* **Analysis Tool:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Source:** Google Play Store Dataset
* **Techniques:** Power Query (M), Data Modeling, and DAX (Data Analysis Expressions).

---

1. Download the `GooglePlayStore 3 .pbix` file.
